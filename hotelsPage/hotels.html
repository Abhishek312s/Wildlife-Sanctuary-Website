Bordehi Wildlife

Hotels in Bordehi

The ever popular wildlife Tourist destination Bordehi WIldlife Santaury was established by noted hunter and naturalist Edward James Bordehi. Giving shelter to India's major population of Tigers and majestic elephants, Bordehi wildlife santaury is a most sought after landing place for the wildlife buffs.... While Corbett Tour Packages gain popularity through the months of November - June but it's never a bad time for a Corbett Tour. Different species of Deer, Sambar, wild boar, and leopard and of course the Royal Bengal are the star attraction of the park. A single Bordehi Tour Package might not ensure a tiger sighting so do not get disappointed at that point of time. Opt for another pocket friendly Bordehi Package and keep alive your wildlife enthusiasm in your heart. We offer plethora of options, you need to pick of your own choice from our extensive database and enjoy the most cherished moment of your life.
